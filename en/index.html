What are microgreens?

Microgreens are small shoots of vegetable and herb plants harvested early. They are simple to use: snip over your sandwich, salad, or smoothie. Pizza, pasta, pesto — almost everything gets better with microgreens.

Pro tip: Living microgreens last long in the fridge (and reduce waste).

Why “living”?

We always deliver microgreens living in their grow medium, so you harvest them yourself. This gives the highest nutritional value, best quality, and longest shelf life.

Packaging: reusable + industrial compostable (when they are done).

How we grow

  • Small-scale production with minimal waste and environmental impact.
  • Hydroponic systems in custom-built solutions.
  • Grown in hemp mats (soil-free).
  • Energy-efficient LED lighting.
  • Sanitation with steam, hydrogen peroxide, and biodegradable detergent.
Why we care

We believe more food should be produced locally, close to the city — by someone you can say hello to. Local production makes it easier to have insight and set requirements.
