We grow microgreens in the basement.

Bladverkstaden is a small-scale producer of living microgreens in Malmö. We want to make it easier to eat healthy, local, and with less waste.

Location & Visit

Augustenborgsgatan 18, Malmö.

Want to see the farm? Get in touch and we'll book a visit.

What we grow

We grow various types of microgreens (shoots of vegetables and herbs).

  • Radish, broccoli, red cabbage
  • Cilantro, mustard, mizuna
  • …and more varieties over time

How we work (briefly)

Soilless

Hydroponic systems, growing in hemp mats.

Circular

Living delivery, compostable/reusable packaging, less waste.
