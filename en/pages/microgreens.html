What are microgreens?

Microgreens are the first phase of a plant's life. It is the stage between sprout and "babyleaf". Despite their size, they are packed with flavor, color, and nutrition.

Flavor explosion

Many are surprised by how much a single small stem can taste. Radish tastes peppery, cilantro tastes intensely citrusy, and peas taste sweet and fresh.

This makes them perfect flavor enhancers, not just decoration.

Nutrition bombs

Research shows that microgreens can contain between 4 and 40 times more nutrients (vitamins and antioxidants) than the mature plant, per gram.

Since the plant is in an intensive growth phase, it is loaded with everything it needs to grow huge.

How to use them?

  • On sandwiches: Replace (or complement) the lettuce and cucumber.
  • In salads: Mix in a handful of pea shoots or sunflower shoots for crunch.
  • Topping: Perfect on pizza, pasta, soups, or tacos.
  • Smoothies: Blend in without overpowering the flavor (e.g. sunflower).
Tip: Harvest and eat immediately

Since our microgreens are delivered living, you cut them just before eating. This keeps their crispiness and nutrition at max.
